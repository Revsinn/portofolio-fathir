About

A bit about me

Hi there! I’m Fathir Alfahrezi, a passionate student diving deep into the world of Information and Technology. I enjoy understanding how things work behind the scenes — from how data flows through a system to how users interact with an interface.

Right now, I’m studying Informatics Engineering at Universitas Islam Negeri Jakarta, where I’m building a strong foundation in programming, software development, and problem solving. Coming from a technical vocational background in automotive engineering, I’ve learned to think systematically, pay attention to detail, and stay disciplined when working on complex systems.

Outside of code, I explore videography, photography, and digital editing as creative outlets. This combination of technical and creative skills helps me design experiences that are not only functional, but also visually engaging and meaningful for the people who use them.
